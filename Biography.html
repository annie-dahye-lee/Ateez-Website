Biography

Ateez (Korean: 에이티즈, pronounced "Ay-Teez"), is an 8-member boy group from South Korea under the company KQ Entertainment. Despite their short time since debut, they are incredibly popular internationally and has gained the attention and love of many people all over the globe. The fandom name for Ateez is called "Atiny" (Ateez + Destiny).
