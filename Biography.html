Biography

Ateez (Korean: 에이티즈, pronounced "Ei-Teez"), is an 8-member boy group from South Korea under the company KQ Entertainment. They debuted on November 2, 2018- their first performance grabbed the attention of many fans even on the first day. Despite their short time since debut, Ateez huge worldwide has several acheivements, making them the biggest rookie group of the year. They are incredibly popular internationally and has gained the attemntion and love of many people all over the globe.
